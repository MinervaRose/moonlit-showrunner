# Moonlit Showrunner — Building an AI Short-Drama Pipeline

Moonlit Showrunner explores a simple question: can an AI system behave less like a text generator and more like a small creative production team?

The prototype takes a short emotional premise and expands it into a complete production package: story brief, script, storyboard, visual prompts, continuity report, edit decision list, and a final MP4 assembled from generated title and scene cards.

The project is deliberately modest in scope. It does not try to replace filmmakers, writers, or visual artists. Instead, it explores how structured AI workflows can support early-stage story development and previsualization.

The first demo uses a magical tooth fairy story because it is emotionally clear, visually rich, and suitable for short-form video. The aim is to demonstrate a coherent pipeline rather than a one-shot prompt.
